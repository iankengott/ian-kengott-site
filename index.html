07Principles

Build, verify, simplify.≈ 2 min

The operating rules I try to keep — taste, restraint, and respect for the work.

EngineeringProcessEthicsOperations
“
01

Own the stack.

Engineering

Prefer tools that can be inspected, moved, and repaired.

Source-available, self-hostable, and fork-friendly by default.

02

Make it useful early.

Process

A rough working system teaches more than a polished guess.

Ship the thinnest honest version first; refine under real load.

03

Stay grounded.

Ethics

Research pages should distinguish confirmed work from context and avoid inflated claims.

Label speculation as speculation. Cite or qualify.

04

Protect the private parts.

Operations

Public work should show taste and direction without leaking operational details.

Share the shape of the work, not the keys to the lab.

05

Automate after understanding.

Process

The best automation starts with knowing where the friction really is.

Run the workflow by hand until the bottlenecks are obvious.
